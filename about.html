About Us

GEC Jamui

Government Engineering College(GEC), Jamui is a government engineering college in Jamui district of Bihar. It was established in the year 2018 under Department of Science and Technology, Bihar. It is affiliated with Aryabhatta Knowledge University and approved by All India Council for Technical Education. College have four branches in Bachelor of Technology course with annual intake of 60 students in each branch. Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering.

Nitish Kumar, Hon'ble Chief Minister

Sumit Kumar, Hon'ble Minister Science & Technology

History

Government Engineering College(GEC), Jamui is a government engineering college in Jamui district of Bihar. It was established in the year 2018 under Department of Science and Technology, Bihar. It is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University and approved by All India Council for Technical Education.

Affiliation

The Institute is academically governed by Aryabhatta Knowlege University, Patna which is the degree awarding authority for the institute as B.Tech. and is recognized by the A.I.C.T.E., New Delhi, and the Government of Bihar State.

Vision of the institution

Government Engineering College, Jamui envisions itself as a pace-setting college of academic excellence centered on education, research and holistic development in existing and emerging field.

Mission of the institution

We are bounded to impart excellent professional education to conduct industry oriented learning and to provide credible consultancy and extension services as per the global socio-economic needs. We have promised to enhance and enrich the teaching/learning process and set such a parameter that other institutes would want to emulate. Being entirely learner centric, college, we aim to promote holistic development, intellect and personality of our students, so that our students can become worthy citizens and highly sought-after professionals worldwide. As a home of learning, college aims to empower the faculty and staff so that the institutes’ ambience can develop mutual respect, cooperative endeavour and openness towards constructive ideas.
